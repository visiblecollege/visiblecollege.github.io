The Visible College welcomes applications from scholars, researchers, and serious independent investigators who share our commitment to rigorous, open-minded inquiry into anomalous phenomena.

#### Who we are looking for

Membership is open to people across all academic disciplines and career stages. We look for:

- **Intellectual honesty** — a willingness to follow evidence where it leads, without predetermined conclusions
- **Relevant expertise** — academic credentials, professional experience, or demonstrated research in a relevant field
- **Collaborative spirit** — openness to working across disciplines and engaging with perspectives different from your own

We particularly welcome scholars from fields that have been underrepresented in this space: medicine, law, engineering, indigenous studies, psychology, and the social sciences.

Membership is by application and review. To express your interest, please complete the form below. We review applications on a rolling basis and will follow up directly.
