The Visible College is an entirely volunteer initiative. Our members contribute their time, expertise, and institutional resources because they believe this work matters — and because they believe the moment for it has arrived.

If our mission resonates with you, there are several ways to support us:

---

#### **Funding**

We welcome contributions that help us sustain and expand our research, educational programming, and public engagement. If you are interested in discussing funding or sponsorship, please [contact us](/contact).

---

#### **Collaboration**

Are you a researcher, institution, or organization working on adjacent questions? We are actively seeking collaborators across disciplines. Partnerships with universities, research centres, policy institutes, and civil society organizations are all welcome.

---

#### **Amplify our work**

Share our publications, articles, and public statements with colleagues and networks who might find them valuable. Rigorous, credible voices in this space are still relatively rare — visibility matters.
