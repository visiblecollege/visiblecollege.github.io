Anthony is a researcher in the [metacrisis](https://civilizationresearchinstitute.org/) space and a community organizer, experiencer, and podcaster in the UFO/UAP and consciousness field. As co-host of the [Fire In The Cosmos](https://www.patreon.com/c/fireinthecosmos) podcast and a member of The New Paradigm Institute's [Citizens for Disclosure](https://newparadigminstitute.org/take-action/join-citizens-for-disclosure/), he's focused on legitimizing the discourse around anomalous phenomena and extraordinary human experience.

Anthony is a 1st-generation immigrant from Lebanon and holds a Master of Public Policy from George Mason University and a B.A. in International Studies from Virginia Tech. In his former career, he spent 15 years working as an open-source intelligence analyst with U.S. government contractors.
