#### Media enquiries

We welcome media enquiries and are happy to connect journalists with appropriate members of our team. Please include a brief description of your publication, the topic you are covering, and your deadline.

#### Collaboration

If you are a researcher, institution, or organization interested in collaborating with The Visible College, please describe your work and how you see the potential connection. We review all proposals seriously.

#### Membership

If you are interested in joining The Visible College as a member, please use the [Membership](/membership) application form rather than this contact address.
